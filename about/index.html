About

We watch prices so you never overpay.

Vigil is a price-intelligence tool for deal hunters and smart shoppers. You tell Vigil what you want and the price you'd pay; Vigil checks it around the clock and alerts you the moment it drops to your target — over Discord, email, Telegram, or SMS. No refreshing, no FOMO, no buying at a fake “sale” price.

Why we built it

Online prices move constantly, and “sales” are often just the regular price wearing a costume. Vigil records every price check, so you can see the real trend over time and buy at the actual bottom instead of the marketing one. It's the price history a retailer would rather you didn't have.

How Vigil makes money

We're upfront about this: Vigil earns money two ways. First, paid plans (Pro and Elite) for people who track more products and want priority checks. Second, affiliate commissions — when you buy something through a link on Vigil (for example an Amazon or Booking.com link), we may earn a small commission. It never costs you anything extra, and it never changes which deals we show you or how we rank them. A price drop is a price drop whether we earn a commission or not.

See our full Affiliate Disclosure for the details.
